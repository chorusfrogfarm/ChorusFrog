Your pledge for 2027
We are reaching about 100 households with this survey. If every household pledged around $500/yr we would be able to provide every household with an abundant box of veggies every other week for 20–25 weeks. If every household pledged $100 we would be able to provide a bag of storage crops (potatoes, carrots, beets, squash, garlic, onions) every month for 6 months. If 10 households pledged $1,000 we could grow one or two low maintenance crops, like garlic and potatoes to share.

Consider 1% of your gross annual income or 10% of your annual food budget.

Your responses go directly to the Chorus Frog Farm team.
